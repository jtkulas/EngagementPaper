Engagement Survey Technical Report

E Qualitative item characteristics

Item reading level information was performed via the package quanteda version 3.0.0 (Benoit et al., 2021) in R version 4.1.0 (2021-05-18). Two indices were investigated: “Flesch-Kincaid” is the same grade level index that’s currently used by Microsoft Word (Kincaid et al., 1975) whereas “Dale.Chall” reflects Nwd [“difficulty” of words; Chall & Dale (1995)]. These indices are primarily influenced by two qualitative characteristics: Nwf, which is the number of words matching the Dale-Chall List of 3000 “familiar words,” and Nwd, which is the number of “difficult” words not matching the Dale-Chall list of “familiar” words.

The average Flesch-Kincaied (e.g., reading grade) was 6.2 (sd = 3.04). The average Dale-Chall index was 38.61 (sd = 11.11).
